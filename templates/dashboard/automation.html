Automation Tips & Best Practices
Prepare Your Data

Create an Excel file with proper column headers like "name", "email", "phone", etc. Make sure your data is clean and formatted correctly.

Personalize Your Messages

Use placeholders like {name} in your templates to personalize messages. Personalized messages have higher engagement rates.

Timing Matters

Send your messages at optimal times when recipients are likely to be available. Avoid late night or early morning sends.

IVR Integration

Set up IVR webhooks to automatically capture call data and associate with leads. This helps in tracking customer interactions effectively.
